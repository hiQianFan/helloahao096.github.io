# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, technical readers, AI/search users, and the site owner. They usually arrive from search, shared links, or AI tools and want direct practical guidance, source Markdown, and project context without marketing friction.

## Product Purpose

QianFan is a personal VitePress knowledge site for technical notes, project writeups, investment notes, and personal pages. Success means articles are easy to scan, easy to share, easy for search and AI systems to understand, and pleasant to read on desktop and mobile.

## Brand Personality

Practical, technical, transparent.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page polish, decorative card grids, overbuilt UI chrome, and visual effects that compete with reading. The site should not feel like a template or a marketing funnel.

## Design Principles

- Reading first: article content stays primary; tools and meta controls support it quietly.
- Source-friendly: expose clean Markdown and machine-readable indexes where useful.
- Compact but clear: metadata and actions should align cleanly without creating extra visual sections.
- Preserve the terminal-green identity without reducing contrast or readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe interactions, and mobile touch targets large enough for one-handed use.
